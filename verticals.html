Vertical Axis

Eight verticals. One substrate.

Phronesis is an AI-native forecasting substrate instantiated for energy-transition first. Roughly seventy percent of the architecture is vertical-agnostic; the per-vertical layer specializes the prompt library, the data plane, and the canonical-correctness contracts. Five verticals live on production today; three more are pre-launch ratified-form-ready.

V#1

Energy Transition

Live

Direct energy forecasts on generation capacity, demand, infrastructure events, climate-fund allocation. The founding vertical of Phronesis. Customer class: capital allocators in energy infrastructure, utility and grid operators, energy-sector corporate strategy, climate-aligned funds.

StatusProduction since 2026-04-24

  • E3 Interconnection queue throughput and project-completion probability
  • E4 Critical-minerals supply chain availability for energy-storage build
  • E5 FERC ruling and state PUC docket outcome probabilities

Cross-vertical intersections

Intersection forecasts are first-class.

The Phronesis Cross-Pollination Map is a first-class architectural artifact. Forecasts that cross verticals — V#1×V#2 grid-compute, V#5×V#7 space-regulatory, V#2×V#8 compute-robotics, V#1×V#4 energy-climate — are not bolted on. They are architected at the substrate layer and minted with the same canonical-quantile (p10/p50/p90) discipline as direct-vertical forecasts.

The Phronesis Grid-Compute Forecast (V#1×V#2 intersection at the I1 archetype) is the flagship cross-vertical product. AI compute power demand intersected with grid-capacity scenarios. First-mint attested at $0.0133 on 2026-04-26 — the first cross-vertical intersection forecast in Phronesis history.
