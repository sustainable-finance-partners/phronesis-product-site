What Phronesis is

The neutral assurance substrate that agentic markets need before machines can be trusted with real decisions.

Forecasting is the entry primitive, not the product. Frontier models are rapidly internalizing forecast generation; that is a supply-side improvement, not a substitute. The defensible layer is what no single model call contains: neutrality (a forecaster cannot score itself), persistent memory (a resolved-outcome ledger that compounds on wall-clock time), accountability (auditable, legally-defensible decision packets), and the standard (whoever defines how decision quality is scored). Phronesis is that layer.

The brand name is deliberate. Aristotle distinguished phronesis — practical wisdom — from episteme (systematic knowledge) and techne (craft). Phronesis is the wisdom that guides judgment in domains where rules cannot be mechanically applied. The platform names that function precisely.

The atomic product: the Decision Asset.

A Decision Asset is a machine-readable, auditable decision packet that an agent or enterprise can cite, reuse, dispute, and settle against. Ten fields, one packet:

  • forecast_distribution — probability + horizon + scenario branches (not just yes/no)
  • evidence_graph — primary-source chain + recency + source weight + contradiction flags
  • methodology_hash — version-pinning + reproducibility anchor
  • calibration_grade — calibration class + per-class historical performance
  • baseline_comparison — Phronesis vs naive vs market vs expert vs LLM-agent baselines
  • mandate_scope — what the requesting agent is authorized to do (AP2 mandate metadata)
  • counterparty_passport — trust profile of any agent, seller, API, or data source involved
  • action_boundary — what executes automatically vs what requires human ratification
  • settlement_hook — what future event resolves this Decision Asset
  • audit_receipt — content-addressed, immutable provenance anchor — the basis for Proof of Decision

Every mint is content-addressed, persisted to the Mnemosyne ledger, and rendered publicly on the calibration scorecard from the moment it issues. Proof of Decision is the publicly-verifiable form of the audit receipt — agents, regulators, and counterparties can independently confirm any Decision Asset's content and provenance.

Architecture

Six primitives. N applied verticals. One substrate.

The architecture is a matrix: six foundational horizontal primitives, applied across twelve vertical markets, with V#0 AI Industrial Mobilization & Situational Awareness as the meta-vertical. "Six primitives, many markets" — not "twenty unrelated verticals."

P1

Identity & Mandate

Verifies agent identity, authority, scope, budget, and permitted action class. AP2 mandate verified at a blocking middleware gate; the substrate enforces the boundary.

P2

Reputation & Track Record

Scores agents, MCP servers, sellers, APIs, and data providers against outcomes. The Counterparty Passport surfaces the live trust profile; every interaction compounds the substrate.

P3

Forecast & Evidence

Calibrated, cited, scored probability assets. This is the Decision Asset substrate — every mint primary-source-cited, time-stamped, and rendered on the public scorecard.

P4

Coordination & Negotiation

Multi-agent coordination primitives — auction, bid, match, route, coalition formation. Concrete first surface: the Power-to-Compute RFQ workflow.

P5

Insurance & Outcome

Prices residual risk, guarantees, outcome-conditional terms. Outcome-conditional pricing tier; integrates with the Mandate Risk Score pre-transaction control point.

P6

Market-State Telemetry

Live activity across endpoints, rails, models, marketplaces, counterparties. The agentic-marketplace microstructure feed — the substrate that lets agents route on present-tense market truth.

P1, P2, P3, P6 are LIVE on production today. P4 and P5 are planned — named here per public roadmap discipline, not over-promised as shipped.

Substrate discipline

Four commitments enforced at the code layer.

Not asserted in marketing copy. Verified by the codebase.

01

Primary-source citation always

Every Decision Asset's evidence_graph traces to primary sources: FERC filings, EDGAR, USPTO, state PUC dockets, DOE grant awards, peer-reviewed research, satellite telemetry, IFR World Robotics disclosures. No second-hand citations of secondary citations.

02

Timestamp discipline

Every Decision Asset carries an issue date and an evaluation window. The Mnemosyne ledger is the canonical record of what was known when. Calibration accrues publicly over time. Past performance is observable; no cherry-picking.

03

MCP and REST co-equal

Every endpoint that exists for human-driven REST clients exists at parity for autonomous-agent MCP clients. Idempotency keys required, not optional. Errors structured with stable machine-recoverable codes.

04

Sub-cent meter event precision

Per-call pricing reports inference cost-attestation with Decimal precision. Stripe meter events fire at canonical persistence to the Mnemosyne ledger. No floating-point drift in the billing path.

05

AP2 mandate at the door

Autonomous agents authenticate via Google Agent Payments Protocol mandates verified at a blocking middleware gate. Mandate signature verification budget is approximately two to five seconds. Agents act within mandate scope; the substrate enforces the boundary.

06

Per-tenant isolation at the substrate

Cross-tenant data access is architecturally prevented at the boundary publish layer. Customer queries never see other customers' data. The isolation is enforced by the code path itself, not by configuration or policy.

The moat

Market Memory — the calibration ledger that compounds on wall-clock time.

The long-term moat is not the model, not the API, not the UI — it is the resolved-outcome ledger. Every Decision Asset that resolves adds a row to a track record that no competitor can shortcut. Calibration history compounds per vertical, per horizon class, per methodology version. Counterparty Passport history compounds across every cross-agent interaction. Dispute and adjudication history compounds with every resolution. Cross-vertical priors strengthen adjacent verticals via the shared substrate.

Phronesis Market Memory is the durable asset. The substrate-investment we make today is not consumed at delivery; it compounds toward the moat the agentic economy will need in 2030 and beyond.
